## SECTION 1 — COMPANY PHILOSOPHY
ROYALTÉ MASTER CONSTITUTION v1.0
🔒 LOCKED — BOARD APPROVED

1.0 Purpose
Royalté exists to make the invisible business infrastructure of music visible.
While the music industry has invested heavily in discovery, promotion, streaming, and audience analytics, the operational systems that govern ownership, metadata, publishing, rights administration, royalty collection, identity management, and catalog integrity remain fragmented and poorly understood.
Royalté exists to bridge that gap.
Its purpose is to provide creators with the intelligence required to understand, manage, protect, and optimize the business infrastructure behind their music.
Royalté believes every creator deserves visibility into the systems that determine how their work is identified, administered, tracked, and monetized throughout the world.

1.1 The Foundational Belief
Exceptional music careers should not be limited by invisible infrastructure failures.
Talent creates music.
Infrastructure protects value.
Royalté exists because business infrastructure has become too complex for creators to manage without intelligent assistance.
The future of music ownership depends not only on creation, but on understanding the systems that support it.
Royalté believes creators should possess the same level of visibility into their business infrastructure as they possess into their creative output.
Backend intelligence is a creator right, not a luxury.

1.2 The Problem Statement
Modern music exists inside a global network of disconnected platforms, databases, collection societies, metadata standards, distributors, publishers, and rights organizations.
Most creators have little visibility into these systems.
As a result, identity inconsistencies, incomplete metadata, missing registrations, fragmented catalogs, administrative gaps, and operational blind spots become common throughout the industry.
Royalté exists to transform this complexity into clear, actionable intelligence.

1.3 Mission
To empower every music creator with the intelligence needed to understand and manage the business infrastructure behind their catalog.
Royalté seeks to make Music Backend Intelligence™ as essential to a music career as streaming analytics are today.

1.4 Vision
Royalté will become the global intelligence platform for music business infrastructure.
The company will provide creators, managers, labels, publishers, estates, investors, and rights holders with continuous visibility into the operational health of music catalogs.
Royalté's long-term objective is to establish Music Backend Intelligence™ as a globally recognized category.

1.5 Category Philosophy
Royalté is not a distributor.
Royalté is not a publisher.
Royalté is not a performing rights organization.
Royalté is not a collection society.
Royalté is not a streaming service.
Royalté is not a royalty recovery company.
Royalté exists as the intelligence layer that helps creators understand and navigate the entire backend music ecosystem.
The category is Music Backend Intelligence™.
Everything Royalté builds must reinforce ownership of that category.

1.6 Company Principles
Intelligence Before Assumption
Royalté prioritizes observable evidence over speculation.
Recommendations should be grounded in data whenever possible.

Simplicity Before Complexity
Backend systems are inherently complicated.
The user experience should never be.
Royalté absorbs complexity so creators do not have to.

Education Before Selling
Every interaction should increase the user's understanding of their music business.
Education creates trust.
Trust creates adoption.
Adoption establishes category leadership.

Transparency Before Mystery
Users deserve to understand why findings exist.
Scores must be explainable.
Recommendations must be understandable.
The platform should never operate as an unexplained black box.

Prevention Before Recovery
Preventing infrastructure failures is more valuable than repairing them after they occur.
Monitoring should always be prioritized over reactive investigation.

Trust Before Revenue
The long-term success of Royalté depends upon credibility.
Short-term conversions must never compromise long-term trust.

Infrastructure Before Promotion
Promotion creates attention.
Infrastructure protects value.
Royalté focuses on protecting value.

1.7 Product Philosophy
Royalté should simplify rather than replace.
The company should not attempt to duplicate existing industry participants.
Instead, Royalté should provide intelligence across all participants.
Royalté should:
• Analyze
• Explain
• Monitor
• Interpret
• Connect
• Recommend
• Educate
The platform exists to improve understanding rather than ownership of infrastructure itself.

1.8 User Philosophy
Royalté serves creators at every level of their career.
From independent artists releasing their first single to institutional catalog owners managing thousands of assets, every user deserves clear, understandable intelligence about their music business infrastructure.
The platform should assume curiosity rather than expertise.
The platform should educate without overwhelming.

1.9 Product Decision Framework
Before any feature enters development, it must answer the following questions:
Does this increase visibility into backend music infrastructure?
Does this reduce user confusion?
Does this create meaningful intelligence?
Does this improve long-term catalog health?
Does this reinforce Royalté's ownership of the Music Backend Intelligence™ category?
Does this simplify the platform rather than increase unnecessary complexity?
If any answer is negative, the feature should be reconsidered or rejected.

1.10 Artificial Intelligence Philosophy
Artificial Intelligence exists to amplify human understanding.
AI should explain rather than obscure.
AI should simplify rather than complicate.
AI should increase confidence rather than dependency.
Royalté AI agents exist to interpret music business infrastructure and present actionable intelligence to creators.

1.11 Constitutional Standard
Every product, report, audit, dashboard, AI agent, educational asset, monitoring service, API integration, engineering decision, and strategic initiative produced by Royalté shall be evaluated against this philosophy.
If any future decision conflicts with these principles, these principles shall take precedence unless formally amended through Constitutional Board Review.

1.12 Constitutional Guardrails
Royalté shall remain platform-agnostic.
Royalté shall never own creator rights.
Royalté shall never participate in royalty collection as principal.
Royalté shall never monetize creator confusion.
Royalté shall exist to increase creator understanding before increasing revenue.

1.13 AI Governance Principle
All present and future Royalté AI agents shall inherit the philosophy and constitutional principles contained within this Constitution.
No AI agent may contradict or supersede the Constitution.

1.14 Simplicity Principle
Every release should make Royalté easier to understand than the previous release.
Complexity belongs inside the platform.
Clarity belongs to the user.

1.15 The North Star Metric
The ultimate measure of Royalté's success is not subscriptions, revenue, traffic, or valuation.
The ultimate measure of success is the degree to which creators understand, control, and optimize the business infrastructure behind their music.
Revenue follows trust.
Trust follows understanding.
Understanding follows intelligence.

### 8B.1 The Seven Layers

```
1.  Providers                              (Spotify · Apple Music · MusicBrainz ·
                                            Discogs · YouTube · MLC · etc.)
        ↓
2.  Normalization Adapters                 lib/publishing/*-adapter.js
        ↓
3.  Royaltē Identity Graph™                api/_lib/identity-graph.js
        ↓
4.  Canonical Intelligence Assembly Engine™ api/_lib/cio-assembler.js
        ↓
5.  Canonical Intelligence Object™ (CIO)   (deep-frozen artifact)
        ↓
6.  Royaltē Rule Library™                  api/rules/*
        ↓
7.  Royaltē Intelligence Engine™           api/_lib/intelligence-engine.js
        ↓
    Observations · Recommendations · Risks · Strengths · Opportunities · Coverage
        ↓
8.  Consumers                              Mission Control™ · Executive Brief™ ·
                                            Health™ · Monitoring™ · Revenue Signals™ ·
                                            APIs · AI Agents
```

### 8B.2 Layer Ownership

| # | Layer | Owns | Locked At |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Providers** | Raw third-party responses | (external) |
| 2 | **Normalization Adapters** | Translation of one provider's responses into Royaltē-canonical objects. No business logic. | `mlc-publishing-adapter-v1.0` (Phase 2, 2026-06-10) |
| 3 | **Royaltē Identity Graph™** | Cross-platform relationships: artists, compositions, writers, recordings, link maps. The graph is the sole owner of relationship state. | Phase 3 (`bf12b5a`, 2026-06-10) |
| 4 | **Canonical Intelligence Assembly Engine™** | Deterministic projection of (graph + adapter outputs + scan payload) into the CIO. Pure function. No I/O. No domain reasoning. | Phase 4 (`a3c78d7`, 2026-06-10) |
| 5 | **Canonical Intelligence Object™ (CIO)** | The deeply-frozen artifact representing Royaltē's current understanding of an artist. Summarizes — never duplicates graph storage. References (royalteId, writerIPI) only. | (data shape — locked with Phase 4) |
| 6 | **Royaltē Rule Library™** | Declarative business knowledge — pure-data rule objects with `(cio) → boolean` conditions. Never executes. Never imports provider field names. | Phase 5 (`8907bd6`, 2026-06-11) |
| 7 | **Royaltē Intelligence Engine™** | Generic, deterministic execution: evaluates every rule against the CIO and projects firing rules into observations, recommendations, risks, strengths, opportunities, and coverage. No category switches. | `intelligence-engine-v1.0` (Phase 6, `a23788b`, 2026-06-11) |
| 8 | **Consumers** | Presentation. Each consumer reads the engine's frozen output and renders it for its surface. No consumer recomputes intelligence. | (per-surface) |

### 8B.3 Constitutional Separation

Three responsibilities, three layers, never mixed:

- **Knowledge** belongs in the Rule Library.
- **Execution** belongs in the Intelligence Engine.
- **Presentation** belongs in consumers.

Any future PR that mixes these — embedding rules inside the engine, computing intelligence inside a consumer, surfacing presentation strings inside a rule — is constitutionally rejected.

### 8B.4 Immutability and Determinism

Layers 4 through 7 are pure functions whose outputs are deeply frozen at the boundary:

- The CIO is frozen the moment the Assembly Engine returns it.
- The Rule Library's `ALL_RULES` and every rule object within it are frozen at module load.
- The Intelligence Engine's output is frozen before return.

Given identical inputs (artist name + sources for the CIO; CIO + rule library for the engine), every layer produces byte-identical output across runs. No randomness. No LLM. No network in the reasoning path.

### 8B.5 Generic Iteration

The Intelligence Engine knows nothing about specific categories, providers, or domains. There is no `switch (category)`, no `if (provider === 'mlc')`, no per-category branch anywhere in the engine. Rules drive everything. Adding a new category, provider, or domain happens only in the Rule Library; the engine doesn't change.

### 8B.6 Engineering Doctrine

- One Adapter per provider. No second normalizer.
- One Identity Graph for the platform. No parallel relationship stores.
- One CIO per scan moment. No alternative aggregations.
- One Rule Library. No engine-side rule duplication.
- One Intelligence Engine. No consumer-side reasoning.

### 8B.7 Future Layers

Reserved sections of the CIO (`monitoring`, `revenue`) and reserved Rule Library categories (`MONITORING`, `REVENUE`, `GENERAL`) carry empty arrays today. Phase 7+ may populate them. When that happens, the change lands in the existing files of layers 5 and 6 — no new layer is added without explicit Board ratification of this Constitution.

### 8B.8 Principle

The Royaltē Engineering Stack™ is what allows Royaltē's intelligence to evolve organically for decades without rewriting the engine. Each layer is replaceable in isolation. Each layer is testable in isolation. Each layer is constitutionally responsible for one thing, and one thing only.

**One Adapter. One Graph. One CIO. One Library. One Engine. One Platform.**
