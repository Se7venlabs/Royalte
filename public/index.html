About this beta

Spotify + Apple Music
Beta.

Royaltē shows you only what is verified. We do not display stream estimates, revenue projections, or any inferred data. Every status you see — Registered, Not Registered, Pending, Not Confirmed — is based on a direct API check.

This beta covers Spotify and Apple Music — paste either platform's artist or track URL and we'll run the audit. Publishing, SoundExchange, and PRO data cannot be verified via public API and are shown as Not Connected or Not Confirmed until you provide that information directly.

Full audits in this beta are delivered manually by our team within 12–24 hours.
