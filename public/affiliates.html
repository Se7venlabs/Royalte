FAQ

Common
Questions.

When do I start earning commission? ▾
Royaltē is free during beta so there's nothing to earn commission on yet. Every qualified referral you send during beta is tracked and attributed to you from day one. When paid tiers launch, those conversions earn commission retroactively at your locked-in 30% founding rate.
How does tracking work? ▾
We use Rewardful, which integrates directly with Stripe. When an artist clicks your unique link, a 60-day cookie is set. If they convert within 60 days — on any device, any visit — the commission is attributed to you automatically. You'll have a Rewardful dashboard showing clicks, conversions, and earnings in real time.
What if an artist hears about Royaltē from me but doesn't click my link? ▾
Artists can also type your referral code manually in the audit request form. We'll ask them to enter a code during signup — if they type your handle, the referral is attributed to you. For edge cases where you can provide evidence (a screenshot of a DM, post, or comment), contact us and we'll review manual attribution.
How and when do I get paid? ▾
Commissions are paid monthly on a NET-15 basis — within 15 days of the end of each calendar month. Payments are made via PayPal, Wise, or bank transfer. The minimum payout threshold is $50 USD. Amounts below that roll over to the following month.
Is the 30% rate really locked in permanently? ▾
Yes — founding affiliates who sign the affiliate agreement during beta lock in 30% for the lifetime of the agreement. The founding rate cannot be reduced without your written consent. This is written into the founding affiliate agreement you'll receive if approved.
Do I need a large audience to apply? ▾
No — audience quality matters more than size. A music educator with 2,000 highly engaged artist subscribers is more valuable to us than a general creator with 200,000 followers. We look at how relevant your audience is to Royaltē, not just the numbers.
What do I have to do to promote Royaltē? ▾
There's no minimum promotion requirement. We'll give you ready-to-use captions, a sample pitch, and your unique link — but how and when you share is completely up to you. We'd rather you promote naturally and honestly than feel obligated to push it.
