About Royaltē

Royaltē is a music intelligence and audit platform designed to help artists uncover potential royalty, metadata, registration, and catalog issues across streaming and music ecosystems.

Think of it as an X-ray for your release backend — surfacing the gaps and inconsistencies that quietly block royalty flow.

Royaltē scans your artist catalog and music data to identify potential problems that may be affecting royalty visibility, registrations, metadata consistency, and platform presence.

The audit covers six modules: metadata integrity, platform coverage, publishing risk, duplicate detection, YouTube and UGC presence, and sync readiness.

03

Full Audits

The Full Audit expands on the free scan with deeper analysis, issue-by-issue explanations, confidence indicators on every flag, and actionable recommendations you can take back to your distributor, publisher, or PRO.

You receive a structured PDF report plus an in-platform dashboard view.

Royaltē uses a tiered confidence system on every flag — HIGH, MEDIUM, LOW, and AUTH_UNAVAILABLE.

HIGH-confidence flags are based on verified data from authoritative sources — ISRC mismatches, missing platform listings, duplicate registrations, and similar verifiable signals.

LOW-confidence flags are inferences worth investigating but not guaranteed. Every flag in your report includes the data source and reasoning so you can verify it independently before taking action.

No. Royaltē provides intelligence and guidance — not collections. Recovery actions must be completed through your distributor, publisher, PRO, or the appropriate collection organization.

Our role is to identify the gaps and tell you exactly who to contact and what to ask. The recovery itself happens through your existing rights infrastructure.

No audit platform can guarantee recovery outcomes. Royaltē highlights potential issues using verified and inferred data signals — but the final outcome depends on your distributor's response, the platform's policies, and the specific circumstances of each flagged item.

What we do guarantee: every flag is traceable, explainable, and accompanied by the reasoning behind it.
