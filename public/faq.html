About Royaltē

Royaltē is the world's first Music Backend Intelligence™ platform — the intelligence layer behind your music.

Streaming services show you plays and followers. Royaltē shows you the business behind them: the registrations, the metadata, the identity records, the coverage gaps, and the changes that actually determine what you get paid.

Think of it as Mission Control™ for your music business.

Every release in your catalog has a public side — what fans see and stream — and a backend side. The backend is where your royalties actually live: ISRCs, ISWCs, splits, registrations, publisher and PRO records, platform identity, and territory coverage.

Music Backend Intelligence™ is the practice of continuously reading that backend so the gaps that quietly cost artists money become visible before they compound.

Royaltē reads it for you, scores it, and tells you what's worth your attention.

Royaltē continuously analyses the infrastructure behind your catalog and surfaces what's working, what's broken, and what may be quietly costing you revenue.

Each scan produces a Royaltē Health Score™ across the dimensions that determine whether your music gets paid correctly — Identity Intelligence™, Publishing Intelligence™, Catalog Intelligence™, Monitoring Intelligence™, and coverage. Mission Control™ keeps those numbers up to date as your catalog evolves and as the platforms behind it change.

What you get is visibility you can't get anywhere else, and a clear list of what to do next.

Royaltē Review™ & Mission Control™

A Royaltē Review™ is the full executive read of your music business backend. It expands on the free scan with deeper analysis across every intelligence engine, the reasoning behind each finding, the confidence supporting it, and clear recommendations you can take back to your distributor, publisher, or PRO.

You receive it as a structured report and as your live Mission Control™ dashboard.

Mission Control™ is your ongoing intelligence dashboard for the business behind your music. It keeps every score, every finding, and every change in one place — and updates as Royaltē continues watching your catalog.

It's the difference between a one-time report and a continuously protected catalog.

Identity Intelligence™ verifies who you are across the platforms that pay you — Spotify for Artists, Apple Music for Artists, YouTube Official Artist Channel, and the registries behind them.

It surfaces duplicate profiles, missing official-artist confirmations, and identity records that don't match across providers. Identity problems are some of the most expensive backend problems in music — and the most invisible.

Publishing Intelligence™ reads what's known about your compositions in the publishing world — MLC registrations, ISWCs, writer credits, and publisher information.

It tells you which works are registered, which are missing, and where the gaps in attribution sit that determine whether mechanical and performance royalties flow to you.
