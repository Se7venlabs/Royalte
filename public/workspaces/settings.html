Royalty Statements™
Royalty statements provide a record of how and when you are paid by the organizations that collect royalties on your behalf. Uploading these statements allows Royaltē to securely organize your historical payment records and, in future releases, power deeper royalty analysis, AI insights, and royalty audit tools.
Why Upload Your Statements?
Many artists receive payments without knowing: whether every song was included, whether every territory reported correctly, whether royalties have declined unexpectedly, whether registrations are complete, or whether payments appear consistent over time. Uploading statements gives Royaltē additional evidence that can improve future royalty analysis and help identify trends or inconsistencies that may warrant further investigation.
Important: uploading royalty statements does not guarantee Royaltē will identify missing royalties or payment errors. These documents provide valuable information that helps improve future AI-powered royalty intelligence and analysis.
Benefits of Uploading Statements
Future Royaltē releases will use these statements to help:
  • Build a historical royalty timeline
  • Compare royalty performance over time
  • Analyze distributor and publisher performance
  • Compare earnings by territory
  • Detect unusual payment trends
  • Support royalty audit workflows
  • Generate ATHENA™ insights
  • Improve Catalog and Publishing Intelligence™
