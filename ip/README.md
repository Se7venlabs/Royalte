## Purpose

The Intellectual Property Vault™ is the authoritative internal corporate record of Se7ven Labs LLC intellectual property — created and maintained to:

1. **Preserve invention history** — a chronological, append-only record of every original idea, breakthrough, and architectural decision so authorship and timing can be re-established at any time in the future.
2. **Document ownership** — establish, in writing, that every asset listed here is owned by Se7ven Labs LLC unless explicitly assigned otherwise.
3. **Establish first-use evidence** — supply the corroborating dates, locations, and asset evidence required to defend common-law trademark rights and contest later filings by third parties.
4. **Support trademark registrations** — give counsel a single source of truth from which to assemble registered, pending, and planned trademark filings across jurisdictions.
5. **Support patent applications** — capture inventor, date, description, and prior-art context for any patentable methodology *before* it is publicly disclosed.
6. **Support copyright protections** — register the creative output (software, writing, video, presentations, brand assets) that automatically attracts copyright under U.S. and Canadian law.
7. **Support licensing negotiations** — list which products and methodologies are available for license, under what terms, and to which counterparties.
8. **Support investor due diligence** — present the IP portfolio in a coherent, auditable form during financing rounds, acquisition discussions, and partner negotiations.
9. **Support legal counsel** — provide outside counsel with an internally consistent record they can rely on without re-collecting context from the founder each time.

---

## Permanence

**The Intellectual Property Vault™ is intended to survive every product lifecycle and shall remain the permanent intellectual property registry of Se7ven Labs LLC regardless of future products, subsidiaries, acquisitions, mergers, divestitures, restructurings, or corporate reorganizations. It serves as the enduring institutional record of ownership, invention history, first-use evidence, strategic intellectual assets, licensing history, and commercialization activity across the entire Se7ven Labs ecosystem.**

## Rules of the Vault

- **Append-only** files: `FIRST_USE_LOG.md`, `PATENTS.md`, `INVENTION_LOG.md`, `FOUNDER_NOTES.md`, `ARCHITECTURE_DECISIONS.md`. Existing entries are never edited or deleted. Corrections are appended as new entries that explicitly supersede the prior one.
- **Confidential** files: `TRADE_SECRETS.md`, `VALUATION.md`. Treat as Se7ven Labs LLC restricted material; do not share outside the Executive Sponsor + Supporting Executives without Board direction.
- **Single source of truth.** Where the Vault and any operating document disagree, the Vault wins for IP claims, ownership, and dates. The Master Constitution still governs principles and architecture.
- **No runtime code.** This directory contains markdown only. No JSON, no JavaScript, no schemas, no tests, no build artefacts.
