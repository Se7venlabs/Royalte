# ARCHITECTURE DECISIONS

**Owner:** Se7ven Labs LLC
**Status:** **APPEND-ONLY.** Existing entries are never edited or deleted. Corrections are appended as new entries that explicitly supersede the prior one.
**Effective:** 2026-06-11

Architectural Decision Record (ADR) log for the Royaltē™ platform. Each row captures a decision the Board (or a delegated executive) ratified, the reason, the alternatives that were considered, the rejected options, the impact, the Board's vote, and the Constitution section the decision supports or amends.

The ADR log is **the** record of *why* the platform is shaped the way it is. When a future executive or counsel asks "why did we decide X?", the answer lives here.

Companion files: `governance/BOARD_DECISIONS.md` (the Board's broader decision register, which includes non-architectural decisions), `INVENTION_LOG.md`, `PATENTS.md`.

## Conventions

- One row per atomic architectural decision.
- `Reason` is the reason at the time of decision — not a later rationalisation.
- `Alternatives` lists what was *considered*; `Rejected Options` lists what was *considered and explicitly declined*.
- `Impact` is the operational change in the platform.
- `Board Vote` records the disposition: `Approved` · `Approved (UNANIMOUS)` · `Conditional Approval` · `Rejected` · `Deferred` · `(operational)` when the decision was operational rather than Board-ratified.
- Rows are **never edited**. Corrections are appended as new rows that explicitly cite and supersede prior rows.
