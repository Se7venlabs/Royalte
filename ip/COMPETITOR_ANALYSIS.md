## Strategic posture

The Royaltē™ category is defended by:

1. **Constitutional architecture** — Knowledge ≠ Execution ≠ Scoring ≠ Presentation. Most adjacents collapse two or more.
2. **Provider isolation** — single-owner adapter rule. Most adjacents mix provider field names into business logic.
3. **Artist-facing trust-tone language** — "not available from reviewed sources" rather than "missing." Most adjacents lean on accusatory framing.
4. **Reconciled Identity Graph as strategic asset** — once populated continuously, the graph itself is licensable.
5. **Public Music Backend Intelligence™ positioning** — first-mover on category framing (see `INVENTION_LOG.md` 2026-05-29).
