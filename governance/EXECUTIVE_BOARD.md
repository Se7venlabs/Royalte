# Royaltē Executive Board

**Status:** roster and constitutional authority of every Executive on the Royaltē Board.
**Authority:** subordinate to `constitution/ROYALTE_MASTER_CONSTITUTION.md`.

This document defines who has authority over what. **Briefs may quote the Board collectively ("Board Directive") or call out a specific Executive when their domain applies.** When two Executives disagree, the matter is escalated to the Founder & CEO. When the Board and the Constitution disagree, the Constitution wins.

**First-draft Board:** the initial role definitions below are first-draft proposals authored at the time the governance layer was established (2026-06-11). The Board may amend any Executive's Title, Responsibility, Constitutional Authority, Scope, or Non-authority via the standard Board Decision process (see `BOARD_DECISIONS.md`). Edits to this file should preserve the structural template.

## Editing this Roster

- **Adding a new Executive:** append a new numbered section using the same template. Record the addition in `BOARD_DECISIONS.md`.
- **Amending an existing Executive's role:** edit in place. Record the change in `BOARD_DECISIONS.md` so historical context is preserved through the append-only decision log.
- **Removing an Executive:** strike through the section's heading (use `~~heading~~`) rather than deleting the section, preserving institutional history. Record the removal in `BOARD_DECISIONS.md`.

Order within this file reflects the seating order of the Royaltē Executive Board at the time the layer was established. The Board may amend ordering via Board Decision.
